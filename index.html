Ever wonder if we can access facebook, tweeter, pinterest and gmail in one tab? Are you tired of the ads and small timelines on your social media page? We've got the solution here by using Neptulla, a social media application that let's you merge your tweeter, facebook, pinterest and gmail in one timeline.
